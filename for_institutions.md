### Workshops:	

* "Telling Your Science as a Story" --- A two hour workshop to learn the basics of adapting narrative storytelling to your scientific story. By the end, participants will have learned the And/But/Therefore (ABT) narrative structure and identified the most compelling storyline for their science.

* "Being an Academic Ally" --- From theis committees to faculty hiring committees, learn how to identify and respond to common biases and altered expectations for marginalized groups to support and champion trainees and peers from these groups.
