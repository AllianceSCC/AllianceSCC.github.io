Sometimes the trick to making science accessible, is just making the time. Need help holding yourself accountable to your writing time? Or want some tips and tricks for making your writing time more productive?

Consider one of our accountability groups!

**Who:** You and up to 9 other academic writers

**What:** Goal setting tips, tricks for making writing time more productive, and writing companions 

**When:** Every weekday for one month, at a time that fits your schedule

**Where:** Dedicated Slack channel

**Why:** To help you make, and meet, your writing goals!
