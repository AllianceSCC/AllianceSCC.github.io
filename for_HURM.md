title: Services for HURM Trainees

To _make science accessible_, Alliance SciComm & Consulting dedicates 10% of our work week to helping HURM<sup>1</sup> trainees succeed. **For free.**


If you belong to one of these groups, and need help with technical writing, grant proposals, or fellowship applications, let us know. You can [schedule](https://calendly.com/alliance_scc/free-consult) a free 15 min consultation to see how we can help. 


<sup>1.</sup>_We define U.S. HURM as individuals belonging to cultures that are either indigenous to North America (e.g. Hispanics or Latinos, American Indians, or Alaska Natives) or who were transplanted to North American for forced labor (e.g., Black or African-American)._
